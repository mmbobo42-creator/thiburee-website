Who We Are

ThibuRee Cleaning (Pty) Ltd is a proudly South African, fully registered and compliant cleaning services company dedicated to delivering excellence in every project we undertake. We specialize in commercial cleaning, industrial cleaning, medical facility hygiene, and specialized cleaning services across South Africa.

We address critical cleaning and hygiene challenges using professional expertise, modern equipment, and strict legislative compliance. Our commitment to quality ensures that every client receives service that meets the highest industry standards.

As a newly established business, we bring fresh energy, dedication, and a commitment to building lasting relationships with our clients.

Our Vision

To become South Africa's most trusted cleaning services provider, recognized for excellence in compliance, innovation, and customer satisfaction. We aim to set the standard for professional cleaning in the commercial, industrial, and healthcare sectors.

Our Mission

To deliver exceptional, reliable, and compliant cleaning solutions that exceed client expectations. We are committed to creating safe, healthy, and pristine environments for businesses across South Africa through professional service delivery and unwavering dedication to quality.
